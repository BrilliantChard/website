KOKWANYO MIXED SECONDARY SCHOOL

SCHOOL MOTTO:
Commitment To Excellence.
SCHOOL VISION:
To Develop a Vibrant Institution which cherishes Progress.
SCHOOL MISSION:
To Promote;
  • Academics,
  • Sports, and
  • Co-curriculum Activities.

CORE VALUES:
  1. INTEGRITY
  2. MORALITY
  3. TOGETHERNESS
  4. HARD WORK
  5. ACADEMIC EXCELLENCE
  6. CO-CURICULUR ACTIVITIES
  7. DISCIPLINE

SCHOOL LOCATION:

Found in:
Homabay County,
Kabondo Kasipul sub - county,
Kokwanyo - Kakelo ward,
Kokwanyo Village,
10 miles from Homabay Township.

HISTORY:

KMSS Was founded by the community members in 2000 as a village school thereafter taken by the government in 2002 to be oficiated as a Public Secondary School.

The school is currently a one of the best performing district schools in Homabay County.

The school occupies 3 acres of land in Kokwanyo Village

The school's main aim is to Enhance Academic Excellence

The compound color is red green

Water (H20) is readily available trhough the taps in the school vicinity
